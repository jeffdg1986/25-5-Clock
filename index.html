About the Pomodoro Clock

The original Pomodoro Clock is an adaptation of a time managment technique developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s. The idea being that people can't (and shouldn't) work like computers. Humans need time to absorb the information they learn and relax their minds. Cirillo showed that roughly a 5 minute break every 20-25 minutes actually results in higher productivity relative to no rest. Take a look at the clock to your right and try it out for yourself.
